About Me

My name is Eslam Mohamed Ahmed, known online as Eslam Looka — a 21-year-old Egyptian ethical hacker specializing in web application and Android penetration testing.

I started my Bug Bounty journey in April 2024 and have since worked with 100+ companies worldwide, uncovering critical vulnerabilities including Race Conditions, Business Logic Flaws, Privilege Escalation, and IDOR issues.
